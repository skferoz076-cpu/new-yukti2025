About Yukti Fest

🔷 TECHNOTSAV YUKTI-2025: Unleashing Innovation and Energy
Hosted by Lingaraj Appa Engineering College, Bidar, the ultimate fusion of innovation, creativity, and competition! Held at the heart of Bidar’s pride — Lingaraj Appa Engineering College — this year’s fest is set to break boundaries and light up the campus with futuristic energy.
🌀 From mind-bending tech challenges to adrenaline-pumping sports events, Yukti isn't just a fest — it's a movement.
Whether you're a code wizard, a creative rebel, or a sports beast, YUKTI-2025 is the platform where your talent gets the spotlight it deserves.
